How Signal Boosting Works

  • 1 - OUTDOOR ANTENNA
    The outdoor antenna receives and transmits signal to nearby cellphone towers. Outdoor antennas can be either directional or omni-directional.
  • 2 - MODEM
    The modem receives the 4G signal. The modem must be set to external antenna mode. You can now connect directly to the modem or connect your wifi router ot the modem
  • 3 - WIFI ROUTER
    The WIFI router makes your 4G Internet connection available to wireless connected devices in your home
